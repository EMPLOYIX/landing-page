The Company

Company Overview

Employix is a workforce technology platform transforming how employment records are created, verified, and used.

We provide a secure, employer-maintained professional identity that enables faster hiring, trusted verification, and seamless workforce mobility — replacing repeated, manual background checks with a single record that is trusted at the source.

Our platform bridges the gap between the parties who create and rely on employment records, uniting them on one verifiable ecosystem built on trust and transparency.

One ecosystem · Five stakeholders
Every party in the hiring chain reads from and writes to the same verified record — so trust is created once and reused everywhere.
